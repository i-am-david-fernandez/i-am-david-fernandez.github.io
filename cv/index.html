Curriculum Vitae

Dr David A. Fernandez

I am a highly proficient and motivated electrical, computer systems, and software engineer who specialises in research and development, with experience in both academic and industrial/commercial environments. Throughout my career, I have maintained focus on what I believe is a good engineer’s underlying goal: to add value by solving real-world problems in an efficient and effective manner.

I have a keen interest in the development of secure systems and technologies that blend mechanical, electrical and electronic systems with software-based computation and the application of computational intelligence to problems in general. In short, I am happiest when solving problems with components in both the real/physical and virtual/computational worlds.
