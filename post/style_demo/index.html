Style Demo

Aug 8, 2019 1 min read
Project

This post simply serves to provide a demonstration of the styles and shortcodes I have added/customised for this site.

Inline Quote

An inline quote can be created via an iquote shortcode pair:

{{< iquote >}}A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step{{< /iquote >}}

Will be rendered as: A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.

Footnotes

Footnotes (the material at the bottom, not the in-body reference) have been tweaked to use a small font size. See the bottom of this post.

Some plain text with a footnote1 inline. An inline quote: A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step2, also with a footnote inline. This second example also (subtly) demonstrates the small, non-breaking space that the iquote shortcode inserts immediately after the closing quotation mark, primarily to avoid the problem of the quotation mark and footnote reference being so close together as to be unclear.
