Biography

I am an Engineer and I love Engineering. Whether in a professional context dealing with software and electrical systems or at home as an amateur gardener setting up a micro spray system for a vegetable garden, I love the challenge of finding and implementing practical, efficient and effective solutions to real-world problems using systematic techniques coupled with creativity.

I have a keen interest in the development of systems and technologies that blend mechanical, electrical and electronic systems with software-based computation and the application of computational intelligence to problems in general.

Professionally, my primary interest is Software Engineering, creating software systems that solve real-world problems and improve reliability, robustness and efficiency through automation. Peripherally, I am interested in helping businesses formalise and automate their workflows and secure their information to help ensure processes are efficient, effective, repeatable and reliable.

Underneath it all, I simply like making things that solve problems, both my own and those of others. In another time, I perhaps may have been a blacksmith.

My hammer is a keyboard, my anvil a workstation, and my steel is an alloy of ones and zeros.

Professional Interests

Areas in which I have a professional interest and enjoy working.

Software Development

I love designing and building software systems, i.e., coding. I am happiest when tackling a problem that can be effectively solved with some as-yet-unwritten software. While I have preferred languages, I’m also keen to learn new languages and tooling, both to expand my skills and ensure I’m using the right tool for the job.

Information Security

I consider it a privilege to be tasked with protecting that which is important, and to me that’s what Information Security is all about, protecting valuable information. I enjoy working on information security both at a product level (i.e., designing and implementing secure software systems) and a business level (i.e., running an Information Security Management System and overseeing GRC-type operations).

Engineering Operations

I am one who values strong foundations, so I enjoy managing system infrastructure, creating and linking together the various parts that form the skeleton of a software-based system. In particular, I enjoy managing such systems via code (i.e., following an infrastructure as code process) coupled with automation of such processes.

Machine Learning & Artificial Intelligence

One of the keys to success is knowing the value of individual components and how to extract that value. There are an increasingly great many tasks for which computers, via Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, reign supreme over their human counterparts, and I enjoy learning about and using these technologies to push boundaries and to do what might previously have been considered impossible.

Automation of Everything!

At the risk of one day putting myself out of work, I love automating. I love the consistency, repeatability and reliability that you can attain when a previously manual process is well-automated. If something is to be done more than once, and the results matter, consider automating.
